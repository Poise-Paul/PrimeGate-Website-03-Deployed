Our Services

At Primegate Properties Limited, we offer a full spectrum of real estate services designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals, investors, and organizations. Our commitment to quality, professionalism, and client satisfaction drives every service we provide:

  1. Home
  2. Services

Property Sales & Marketing

We help clients buy and sell residential, commercial, and industrial properties with ease. Our team provides market insight, valuation, and expert negotiation to ensure successful transactions.

Property Leasing & Rentals

Whether you're looking for short-term or long-term leasing solutions, we connect tenants and landlords through a seamless and efficient process. We also manage tenant screening and lease documentation.

Real Estate Investment Advisory

Our seasoned consultants offer strategic advice to individuals and corporate investors seeking profitable real estate opportunities. We help you make informed decisions backed by market data and trends.

Property & Facility Management

We provide end-to-end property management services including rent collection, maintenance, tenant relations, and asset value enhancement to ensure peace of mind and maximum returns for property owners.

Project Development & Construction

From concept to completion, we develop and deliver residential and commercial projects that reflect quality, sustainability, and modern design. We manage every stage—planning, design, construction, and handover.

Land Acquisition & Documentation

We facilitate secure land acquisition processes and ensure all legal documentation, permits, and titles are handled properly to give our clients confidence and ownership security.

Joint Venture Partnerships

We collaborate with landowners and investors to bring development projects to life, creating mutually beneficial ventures driven by trust, transparency, and shared success.
